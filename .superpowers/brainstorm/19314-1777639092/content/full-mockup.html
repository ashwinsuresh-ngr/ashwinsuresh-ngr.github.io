MCP Servers: A Practical Guide

Model Context Protocol (MCP) changes how AI systems connect to the outside world. Rather than writing bespoke integrations for every tool, MCP gives you a standard interface — one that any LLM-powered agent can speak.

At its core, an MCP server exposes tools, resources, and prompts. Your agent picks up those capabilities at runtime, without you hard-coding anything into the model call itself.

In this post I'll walk through setting up your first MCP server, wiring it to Claude, and the patterns I've found work best in production...
